Title of Article about interesting Visualization

Author of the interesting Article


The new research has shown that there is a consistent difference between the amount of alcohol and beer used by a group of soldiers. These differences could be attributed to:

  • The number of days in the field;
  • The number of slepping time each day;
  • The difference between types of soldiers;
  • Order and discipline.

From the research, we could conclude that a new approach has to be done to limit the amount of alcohol used by soldiers each day, including enhancing discipline, not reducing slepping time each day under 6 hours, and guarantee a comprehensive approach for the eating regime or each person.
